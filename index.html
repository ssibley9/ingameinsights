Built by a coach. For coaches.

InGameInsights makes the tools we wish existed when we were on the sideline ourselves. Generic stat-keeping apps treat every sport like the same spreadsheet — we don't.

Every InGameInsights app is purpose-built for one game. Soccer's possession-first input model is different from basketball's quarter-driven flow, which is different from lacrosse's face-off-and-clear rhythm. The data the coaches care about is different in each. So we built each app from scratch around the way that sport is actually played.

All data stays on your device. No accounts. No cloud sync. No tracking. Your roster is yours, your game data is yours, and the only thing leaving your iPad is the report you choose to share.
