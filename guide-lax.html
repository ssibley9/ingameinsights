How to use LAX '26

Lacrosse scored properly — face-off win rates, shot heatmaps, ground balls, saves, and time of possession, all tracked live from the sideline.

LAX '26 is in field testing with beta coaches this spring. To try it, request TestFlight access and we'll get you set up.
1

Build your roster

Add your players with numbers and positions so face-offs, ground balls, and shots all attribute correctly. Upload your whole roster in bulk, or add players individually.

2

Track face-offs and possessions

Log face-off wins, ground balls, and clears as the ball changes hands. The app keeps a running face-off win rate and time of possession for both teams.

3

Record shots and saves

Mark shot locations to build a heatmap, and log goalie saves as they happen. Patterns in where your shots come from show up fast.

See the shot chart

Shots plot by field location and build into a heatmap, so the hot zones your offense is generating from stand out at a glance.

Goal Missed Shot density
4

Share the report

Export an HTML or CSV report after the game with face-off rates, shot locations, ground balls, saves, and time of possession all in one place.
