How to use Basketball

Basketball stat tracking battle-tested across two seasons and nine teams. Track the game quarter by quarter and watch the box score build itself in real time.

Basketball is now available on the App Store. Download it here to follow along with these steps.
1

Set up your team

Enter your roster with jersey numbers so every stat lands on the right player. You can keep multiple teams if you coach more than one.

2

Tip off a game

Start a new game and track it quarter by quarter, with substitutions and the game clock built into the flow.

3

Log shots and stats

Record makes and misses on the shot chart, plus rebounds, assists, steals, and turnovers, as they happen. The box score builds itself in real time.

4

Review box scores and analytics

Check team and individual analytics live during the game, then dig into the full box score at any point without stopping tracking.

5

Export the box score

Share an HTML or CSV report after the game so players, parents, and assistant coaches can see exactly how it went.
